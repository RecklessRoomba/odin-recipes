Crockpot Meatballs

Description

An easy appetizer, snack, or dinner. It really is a set it and forget it recipe.

What You'll Need

    1 bag of frozen fully-cooked meatballs (32 ounce)
    12 ounces of sweet BBQ sauce of choice
    10 ounces of grape jelly

How to Make It

  1. Pour frozen meatballs into crockpot. Add sauce and jelly.
  2. Cover and cook for 2 to 3 hours on HIGH or 4 to 6 hours on LOW, until sauce is hot.
  3. That's it. Serve it straight from the crockpot, add some toothpicks, really whatever you want.
